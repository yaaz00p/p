Our Mission and Vision

Thika Technical Training Institute was established with the mission of providing technical education and training to empower students with practical skills. Our vision is to be a leading institution in technical education, fostering innovation and excellence.

Thika Technical Training College: Empowering the Future through Quality Education

Thika Technical Training College (Thika TTI) stands as a beacon of excellence in technical and vocational education in Kenya. Established with the mission to provide high-quality, industry-relevant education, Thika TTI is dedicated to empowering students with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in the modern workforce.

Why Choose Thika TTI?

1. Comprehensive Programs: Thika TTI offers a diverse range of programs across various fields, including engineering, computer science, business studies, hospitality, and applied sciences. Our curriculum is designed to meet the evolving demands of the job market, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for their careers.

2. State-of-the-Art Facilities: Our campus is equipped with modern laboratories, workshops, and classrooms, providing an ideal learning environment. We continually invest in cutting-edge technology and resources to enhance the educational experience.

3. Experienced Faculty: At Thika TTI, we pride ourselves on having a team of highly qualified and experienced instructors. Our faculty members are committed to mentoring and guiding students, fostering a culture of academic excellence and innovation.

4. Industry Partnerships: We maintain strong connections with industry leaders, facilitating internships, industrial attachments, and job placements for our students. These partnerships ensure that our training remains relevant and aligned with industry needs.

5. Student Support Services: Thika TTI provides comprehensive support services, including career counseling, academic advising, and extracurricular activities. We aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who are ready to make a positive impact in their communities.

6. Community Engagement: We believe in giving back to the community. Thika TTI actively participates in community development projects and encourages students to engage in social responsibility initiatives.

Join Us Today!

Thika Technical Training College invites prospective students to join our vibrant community and embark on a journey of personal and professional growth. With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and inclusivity, Thika TTI is the ideal place to build a solid foundation for your future career.
